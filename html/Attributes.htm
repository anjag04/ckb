Contradicting Styles

<p align="center">
<hr width="50%" align="left" /></p>

  1. you can have different alignments in each tag of the document.
  2. the inner tag alignment overrides the outer tag alignment.
    In this example <p> is the outer tag and <hr> is the inner tag.
